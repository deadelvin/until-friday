WITCH 102

Intro to English/Language Arts & (Witch)Crafts

Course delves into the realms of spellcraft and linguistic expression. Exploring foundational principles of spell work, from harnessing intent and symbolism to casting spells for manifestation, healing, and transformation. Focus on cultivating a poetic voice, drawing from mystical traditions and personal experiences to craft spells, incantations, and enchanted written works.

Intro to (Witchy) Home Economics

Intro to herbology and potions. Nature, healing, magic, and culinary crafts. This course aims to foster a journey into green and kitchen witchcraft, discovering the magic and wisdom of plants and herbs. Cultivate a magic herb garden, craft herbal remedies and teas with hands-on activities.
