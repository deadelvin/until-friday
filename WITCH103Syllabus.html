Course Overview

This course is an introduction to herbology and potions, focussing on nature and healing. The goal is to cultivate a relationship with your particular environment through hands-on learning.

This is a prerequisite for Advanced Herbal Medicines (WITCH 203)

Learning Outcomes:

Overview of green witchcraft as a nature-based spiritual practice.

Homework:

Research and identify three local plants commonly found in your area and their magical or medicinal properties. Make a list of plants you would like to focus on learning and building a relationship with this semester. Design a garden and plan how you could actually use its produce.
