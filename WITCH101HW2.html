Keith Wrightson states that there were specialists of magic within ten miles of any village: Cunning Men and Wise Women. They had specialized knowledge and inherited power often considered to be passed in the blood. They provided several services, including:

  • Medical (healing w/ herbs and incantations)
  • Diagnosed witchcraft and offered counter-witchcraft

  • Fortune telling
