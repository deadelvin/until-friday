Course Overviews

WITCH 101: This course explores the origins and sociocultural contexts of magic, tracing European traditions from ancient civilizations to modern-day practices. We will examine rituals, symbols, and belief systems through a critical and historical lens.

This course is a prerequisite for Advanced History of Magic: Methodologies (WITCH 201)

WITCH 102: This course delves into the ways the language arts interconnect with magic, recognizing that poetry, symbolism, and linguistic expression are part of the witch's toolkit. We will examine this through a blend of analytic, practical, and creative exercises.

This course is a prerequisite for: Advanced English/Language Arts Magic (WITCH 202), English for the Witch in Summer (WITCH 302), and Advanced Arts & (Witch)Crafts (WITCH 402).

WITCH 103: This course is an introduction to herbology and potions, focussing on nature and healing. The goal is to cultivate a relationship with your particular environment through hands-on learning.

This is a prerequisite for Advanced Herbal Medicines (WITCH 203)
