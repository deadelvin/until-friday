Course Overview

This course explores the origins and sociocultural contexts of magic, tracing European traditions from ancient civilizations to modern-day practices. We will examine rituals, symbols, and belief systems through a critical and historical lens.

This course is a prerequisite for Advanced History of Magic: Methodologies (WITCH 201)
