About Witches

10/6/2025. A creative reflective work on some of Reginald Scot’s The Discoverie of Witchcraft , 1584. Includes what a witch’s traits were expected to be (physically or temperamentally), what a witch was thought to accomplish, some of the ways sexuality emerges in the conversation, and the connection between witchcraft and shapeshifting.

Witchy Services, Sex, and Sorcery

10/15/2025. A reflection on the readings for week 2. Includes naming the main services those who practiced magic historically were known to provide, as well as how Kramer, author of Malleus Maleficarum, gendered magic and explicitly sexualized his witches. Includes a brief reference list for key tenets of magic.
