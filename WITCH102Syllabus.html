Course Overview

This course delves into the ways the language arts interconnect with magic, recognizing that poetry, symbolism, and linguistic expression are part of the witch's toolkit. We will examine this through a blend of analytic, practical, and creative exercises.

This course is a prerequisite for: Advanced English/Language Arts Magic (WITCH 202), English for the Witch in Summer (WITCH 302), and Advanced Arts & (Witch)Crafts (WITCH 402).
